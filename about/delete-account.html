Account and Data Deletion Request

We respect your privacy and guarantee your right to request the permanent deletion of your account and all personal data associated with our application.

How to request deletion:

  1. Open your corporate or personal email client.
  2. Send a message to suportetheblu@gmail.com (or use the button below).
  3. Use the subject exactly as: Account Deletion.
  4. The message must be sent mandatorily from the email address registered in the application to validate your identity.
Request Deletion via Email

What data will be deleted?

  • Profile information (Name, Email, Profile Picture).
  • Activity history and preferences saved in the app.
  • Device identifiers and authentication tokens.

Timelines and Data Retention

Upon receipt of your email, the complete deletion of your data will be completed within a maximum period of 15 business days.

Important Note: Some data may be retained for additional periods if there is a legal, tax or court determination obligation, as provided for by the General Data Protection Law (LGPD) and the Digital Child and Adolescent Protection Act (Lei 15.211).
