Privacy Policy - Registry Pull

This privacy policy applies to the Registry Pull mobile application and any related services operated by The blu (hereinafter referred to as "Service Provider" or "We"). The Service Provider's contact email is suportetheblu@gmail.com.


1. Information Collection and Use

The Application collects information when you download and use it. This information includes:

  • Your IP address and device technical data (such as mobile operating system).
  • The sections of the Application you visit, the time/date and time spent on them.
  • Personal identification data obtained at login (such as Email, unique user ID (UID), Name and Profile Photo).
  • Training Data: Records of calisthenics routines, sets, repetitions, completed training dates, watched videos and body preferences (shoulder, chest, etc.) for calculating statistics and training recommendations.

2. Third-Party Services

The Application uses third-party services that process data according to their own policies. Below are the links to the Privacy Policies of service providers used by the Application:

  • Google Play Services (General operating system services)
  • Firebase (Authentication and Cloud Firestore) (User authentication and cloud training history storage)
  • Google Sign-In (Google account authentication facilitator)
  • RevenueCat (In-app purchase management and Pro subscriptions)

3. Artificial Intelligence (AI)

The Application uses Artificial Intelligence features to analyze your training history and suggest personalized routines based on your level and preferences. All AI processing respects this policy. Your training history is only sent to the intelligent language model after your explicit consent, which can be revoked at any time in the application settings.


4. Your Rights (LGPD / GDPR / CCPA)

We guarantee our users all fundamental privacy rights. You can at any time:

  • Access or correct your personal information.
  • Request complete deletion of your data and account (which can be done immediately and definitively in the "Settings" menu of the application itself, or by contacting us by email).
  • Withdraw your consent for processing data based on prior consent.

5. Data Retention and Deletion
  • User and Training Data: Are securely maintained in Firebase Cloud Firestore while your account is active, allowing synchronization of your workouts on any device.
  • Deletion Flow: If you choose to delete your account via the application, all your identification data and training history will be permanently deleted from our servers.

6. Security

The Service Provider is concerned with safeguarding the confidentiality of your information and adopts appropriate technical and administrative measures to protect your data against unauthorized access or loss.


7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Material changes will be notified through the application or by updating this page with the new effective date.


Contact

If you have any questions about your privacy or about the application's practices, please contact us via email at suportetheblu@gmail.com.

This privacy policy is effective as of July 14, 2026.
